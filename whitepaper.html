The burning system

The burning system works as follows, 0.1% of each transaction will go to the public burning wallet which can be followed from BSC Scan, when 1% of the total supply in the burning wallet is reached, it will start with the burn the BSC and the cycle will continue in the same way, when the total supply of tokens decreases by 13%, the percentage of tokens that will go to the burn wallet will be decreased to 0.08%, when the total supply decreases by 25% the percentage will be close to 0.06%, and so on until reaching very small amounts and then 0.

keep in mind that the system may be subject to change and suggestions for improvement from the community.
